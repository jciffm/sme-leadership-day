Event Vision

Connect. Expand. Build Across Borders.

The Frankfurt Cross-Border Business Summit brings together entrepreneurs, SME leaders, investors, international communities, diaspora networks, expats, policymakers, educators and ecosystem builders who know that sustainable growth is driven by international collaboration and shared value creation.

German Companies

Seeking growth, innovation, talent and access to new markets.

International & Diaspora Communities

Global Partners & Enablers

Facilitating trade, investment, financing, market entry, compliance, logistics and international expansion.
