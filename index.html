Event Vision

Connect. Expand. Build Across Borders.

The summit brings together entrepreneurs, SME leaders, investors, international communities, diaspora networks, expats, policymakers, educators and ecosystem builders who know that sustainable growth is driven by international collaboration and shared value creation.

German Companies

Seeking growth, innovation, talent and access to new markets.

International & Diaspora Communities

Providing cultural intelligence, trusted business relationships, entrepreneurial expertise and direct connections to global markets.

Global Partners & Enablers

Facilitating trade, investment, financing, market entry, compliance, logistics and international expansion.

Main Stage

Program – 25 September 2026

Connecting Frankfurt with global opportunities.

09:00 – 09:45

Registration & Global Networking Coffee

Explore the Networking & Opportunity Hub: regional business corners showcasing global markets, service providers supporting international growth, and ecosystem partners connecting companies, talent and investment.

09:45 – 10:00

Official Opening – Welcome to Frankfurt: Europe's Gateway for Global Business

Opening remarks from JCI Frankfurt, the City Councillor for Economic Affairs and the CEO of IHK Frankfurt on Frankfurt's role as a global business hub.

10:00 – 11:00

Open Dialogue with Politics – "Frankfurt 2031: From Coalition Vision to Global Business Hub"

A strategic conversation on Frankfurt's ambition to become Europe's leading destination for international entrepreneurs, skilled professionals, investors, startups and globally connected SMEs.

11:00 – 11:15

Keynote – "From Made in Germany to Made with Asia"

Building the next generation of global partnerships: new growth markets, evolving supply chains and innovation collaboration between Europe and Asia.

11:15 – 12:00

Interactive Workshop, Part 1 – "Know What You're Looking For: Defining Your Global Opportunity"

Develop your personal Global Opportunity Profile: what you're looking for (capital, partnerships, market entry, customers, know-how, talent, inspiration) and what you can offer – so every conversation counts.

12:00 – 13:30

Networking Lunch & Opportunity Marketplace

Transform conversations into opportunities – connect with regional representatives, trade experts, investors, entrepreneurs and potential collaborators.

13:30 – 14:30

Panel Discussion – Africa: "Powering Digital Innovation: Connecting Cameroon and Germany"

How Cameroon's emerging technology ecosystem and Germany's innovation economy can collaborate through entrepreneurship, investment and diaspora networks.

14:30 – 15:00

Global Networking Break

Continue conversations and engage with regional partners at the Networking & Opportunity Hub.

15:00 – 15:45

Fireside Conversation – "The India–Middle East Corridor: A New Engine of Global Growth"

New trade and investment pathways between Europe, India and the Middle East – and how companies can participate in emerging growth ecosystems. Including audience Q&A.

15:45 – 16:30

Cross-Border Business Connections – "From Conversations to Collaboration"

Facilitated matchmaking based on the Global Opportunity Profiles from the workshop – guided introductions to partners, investors, advisors, customers and talent instead of random networking.

16:30 – 17:00

Closing Conversation – "Lead Beyond Borders: Turning Connections into Partnerships"

How businesses, entrepreneurs and international communities can transform today's conversations into long-term collaboration.

Evening

Global Connections Reception & Interactive Workshop, Part 2 – "Matchmaking Beyond the Event"

An evening networking experience with curated introductions based on your Opportunity Profile – connecting entrepreneurs, investors, SMEs, startups and ecosystem experts over drinks.

Day 2 – 26 September 2026 (optional)

10:00 – 12:30

Discover Frankfurt City Tour

An optional guided tour (approx. 2.5 hours) through Frankfurt's business districts, international communities and cultural landmarks – experience why Frankfurt is one of Europe's leading global cities.

from 13:00

Frankfurt Networking Brunch

Relaxed brunch to continue conversations and deepen the relationships established during the summit.
