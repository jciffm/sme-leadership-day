Good to know

Booking tips

  • Book early: Frankfurt hotel prices rise sharply during trade fairs. Late September can be busy – secure your room as soon as your attendance is confirmed.
  • Getting around: The venue is 2 minutes from Hauptwache, Frankfurt's central S-/U-Bahn hub – any hotel near an S-Bahn station works well.
  • From the airport: S-Bahn S8/S9 to Hauptwache takes about 15 minutes – a taxi is rarely necessary.
  • Staying for the optional program? The Networking Brunch and Discover Frankfurt Tour take place on 26 September – consider booking two nights.
